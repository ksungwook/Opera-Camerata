Opera Camerata

All members of Opera Camerata are professionally educated & trained Opera singers based in the United States. Opera Camerata’s productions are unique, exceptional and special. We stage traditional operas with conductor’s clever, funny & informational storytelling on stage mixed with 21st century technology which will usher the audience into a dream-like performance. Opera Camerata’s innovative & popular outreach program ‘Opera To-Go’ will magically turn your space (church or house) into a perfect opera theater.
