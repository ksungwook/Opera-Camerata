3. Camerata New Jersey

Camerata New Jersey, Founded in 2010, Camerata New Jersey Foundation is committed to bringing the highest performance of classical music to those in the New York metropolitan area as well as developing the next generation of musicians. Through fun and creative programs that we have organized, we strive to interact with people of all ages and backgrounds. We are dedicated to make a positive impact in our community and around the world through music.
